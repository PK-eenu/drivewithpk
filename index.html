Geelong Driving Hotspots

VicRoads test routes originating from South Geelong (Fyans Street) frequently utilize these challenging areas. Review them carefully:

Fyans Street & Moorabool Street Intersections

Watch for lane splitting and quick light shifts during peak hours right after exiting the VicRoads center.

Wilsons Road Roundabout

A multi-lane roundabout requiring proper signals on entry and exit. Make sure your blind spot head checks are visible.

Latrobe Terrace Merges

Accelerate to match traffic speeds (70 km/h) before merging. Ensure smooth mirror checks and head checks.

Did you know? We offer professional mock tests on these exact routes to prepare you completely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about getting your license in Geelong.

To deliver maximum instruction quality and focus, Drive with PK is automatic-only. Automatic driving lets you focus on road awareness and safer defensive driving rather than managing a gearstick and clutch, resulting in faster licensing.

Under Victorian law, if you are under 21, you must log 120 hours of supervised driving (including 20 night hours). The number of professional lessons depends on your starting skill, confidence, and how much practice you get with parents/guardians. Most students take 5 to 15 professional lessons to ensure they correct errors and master the actual test routes.

Our dual-control training vehicles allow PK to intervene and control braking or steering if any safety risk occurs. The training cars are modern, fully insured, and have regular safety assessments. We also clean and sanitize the vehicle cockpit before every lesson for hygiene.

Yes, we provide convenient door-to-door pick-up and drop-off services across all of our serviced suburbs in Geelong. You can start the lesson from your home, school, work, or Deakin Waurn Ponds and end it at another pre-agreed location (within our coverage area).

Yes, we have extensive experience helping overseas license holders adapt to driving on the left side of the road, understanding unique Victorian rules (like hook turns in Melbourne, or local multi-lane roundabouts in Geelong), and successfully passing the VicRoads practical drive test.

We are proud to serve a diverse client base and can offer lessons in English, Hindi, and Malay. If you feel more comfortable receiving instructions or test strategies in Hindi or Malay, please let us know when booking!
